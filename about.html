About Us

Galaxy Trade & Technologies – One of the leading trading concerns with a view to contribute in the field of Surveying, Science, Engineering & Information Technology by importing world renowned brands instruments to drive the country forward.

Our focus is in the market solutions, which consistently offer value in terms of price and quality. We have a team of dedicated professionals with extensive experience, who committed to working effectively provide unparalleled after- sales support, services & calibration to our valuable clients.

We believe in customer satisfaction rather than just selling products.

OUR MISSION

To be an active partner in the development of the country by promoting state of the art technology with a commitment to achieve excellence.

OUR VISION

To be the leading solution provider in every industry we serve, and consistently offer quality products and services.

OUR VALUES

To establish a culture of honesty, integrity and respect for customers and business partners, while providing unparalleled support and services.
